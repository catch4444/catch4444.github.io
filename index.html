About Us

At Catch 44, we plan to create more than just a place to enjoy great brews; we aim to be a community hub, a sanctuary for beer enthusiasts, and a haven for those seeking a taste of exceptional local craft beverages. From classic ales to innovative small-batch creations, our taps flow with an array of flavours to suit every palate. Whether you're a seasoned beer connoisseur or a newbie exploring the world of craft brews for the first time, our knowledgeable staff will guide you on a flavorful journey.

WHAT WE OFFER

Besides a curated selection of handcrafted beers, we also curate a limited no. of extraordinary cocktails, which change every week. Our food menu is designed with care and lots of love, with a focus on being delicious while being simple, wholesome and with a touch of local flavours, to give it the authenticity of the cities we are in. The easy and laid-back ambiance of our cozy taproom invites you to relax, be yourself and just enjoy your beer or drink. We encourage all forms of art, and showcase upcoming small bands,musicians, poets, artists, guest bartenders and even the occasional guest chefs.

OUR BELIEFS

We also encourage people of similar interests to connect over curated spirit tastings, karaoke nights, movie nights, singles nights to mingle with and celebrate the essence of enabling new connections beyond the circle of friends and family.

We also focus on family for the weekend and have kid inclusive weekend breakfasts and brunches where there are curated menus and activities for the kids specially. We take pride in fostering an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome. We are pro LGBTQ and even our team boasts of great inclusivity.
